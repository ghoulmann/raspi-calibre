Time to Restore From

Precise timestamps for stored backup sessions can be found in the Hub's backups section.

Syntax

TIMEAGO := YYYY-MM-DD | YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss | <int>[mhDWMY]

Examples

  2010-08-06           2010, August 6th, 00:00 
  2010-08-07T14:00     2010, August 7th 14:00 UTC 
  6m                   6 minutes ago
  5h                   5 hours ago
  4D                   4 days ago 
  3W                   3 weeks ago 
  2M                   2 months ago 
  1Y                   1 year ago
